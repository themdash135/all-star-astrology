Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

All Star Astrology ("the App") is published by Ernest Taylor Trust. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what controls you have.

What We Collect

We store the birth details and profile information you enter (birth date, birth time, birth location, and optionally your name and Hebrew name) so the App can generate your readings and reopen them later on your device.

How It Is Used

Your data is used to calculate astrology, numerology, and combined reading results. Oracle questions are sent along with your saved reading context so the App can answer from your chart. All calculations are performed on our servers and returned to your device.

What We Share

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data only leaves your device when the App calls the reading or Oracle APIs needed to generate your results. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

Data Storage

Profile details, readings, Oracle history, theme preferences, and motion preferences are stored locally on your device. The App does not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers beyond the duration of an API request.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Controls

You can edit your birth data at any time within the App. Use the "Reset All Data" option in the You tab to remove all saved profile details, readings, theme settings, motion preferences, and Oracle history from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at etbday1@gmail.com.
